Boom

Boom (world: World, position: Types.Vector2f, strength: f32, radius: Meters, falloff: Meters)

Boom creates an explosion in the physics world

Parameters:
  • world: The physics world
  • position: The center position of the explosion
  • strength: The force of the explosion
  • radius: The radius affected by the explosion
  • falloff: How the force decreases with distance

Impulse

Impulse (world: World, body: Obj, impulse: Types.Vector2f)

Impulse Applies a linear impulse to a body at its center of mass.

Parameters:
  • world: The physics world
  • body: The body to apply the impulse to in Ns
  • impulse: The vector force to apply

Torque

Torque (world: World, body: Obj, torque: NewtonMeters)

Torque Applies a rotational impulse to a body.

Parameters:
  • world: The physics world
  • body: The body to apply the torque to
  • torque: The magnitude of the rotational force

Move

Move (body: Obj, pos: Types.Vector2f, rot: Radians)

Move Instantly moves a body to a specific position and rotation. WARNING: This is a VERY EXPENSIVE operation and can disrupt the simulation if overused.

Parameters:
  • body: The body to move
  • pos: The target position
  • rot: The target rotation
